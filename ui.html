AveNew

AveNew came to Zippetomylou Designs in 2014 looking to build their web presence as an Austin, Texas young adults group. AveNew needed a website that could be used at their parent church, Riverbend, to promote the group as well as a place to share information on upcoming events. To solve this issue we included a google calendar api that duplicated AveNew’s events for all to see on the website. Along with the google calendar api, the website also features a simple form allowing for new or current members to inquire information on upcoming events and/or the group. View Design
